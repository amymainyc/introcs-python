STUYFEED

About:

Stuyfeed is a project made by Amy Mai. It is a recreation of some of the quizzes that you would find on the Buzzfeed website. Just choose a quiz, answer the questions, and you will get a result! Click the Stuyfeed button above to go to the main page.



Differences from original proposal:

When writing the original proposal, I hadn't yet figured out what quizzes I was going to recreate. After figuring that out, one of the main differences was less of a dependency on a data source to produce the outcome of the quiz. However, I did add a feature that uses previous scores from other users and calculates how much better or worse you did compared to them.
